setTimeout() is for executing a function 'once' after a specified delay.
//Syntax - setTimeout(function, delay, arg1, arg2,...)
clearTimeout()- to stop the execution of setInterval.


setInterval() is for executing a function repeatedly at specified intervals until explicitly cleared.
//Syntax -  setInterval(function, delay);
clearInterval() - to stop the execution of setInterval.
//Syntax  -clearInterval('name of varible/const in which setInterval stored')
